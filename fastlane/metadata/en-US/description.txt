Agile Africa 2016 Conference | August 22-23 | Johannesburg

This App is your companion to the Agile Africa conference that helps you:
• Navigate the schedule
• See talk and speaker details to help you choose
• Rate the talks (after the talk, please open the talk and tap your rating and optionally add a comment )

Agile Africa is an annual conference where African software professionals meet to uncover better ways of working and developing software.

You will get the chance to hear leading international and local speakers and more importantly, interact with professionals from various software development communities.
